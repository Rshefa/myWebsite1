How to make my comfort food:

When I'm craving something spicy and savory but feel a bit lazy to cook a whole meal, I resort to make a spicy cheesy ramen. It's easy and tastes amazing!

Step 1: Boil the noodles

Grab a Samyang Buldak ramen pack of your choice and boil it in a pot
Should take about 10 min.

Step 2: Chop up some store bought mushrooms and a small onion to add as toppings (optional)

Step 3: Strain the noodles

Step 4: Add some oil in a pan (if you are adding mushrooms & onions)

Add the chopped ingredients and sautee them.

Step 5: On very low heat, dump the sauce and flavor packets

This will be the sauce mixture for the noodles. Don't burn it!

Step 6: add the noodles in and stir till they are entirely covered by the sauce

Step 7: Add 2 slices of cheese on top of the noodles or sprinkle a generous amount of shreded cheese on top

To spice it up, you can top it off with a sunny side up egg.
Now you can enjoy some cheesy and spicy noodles!
